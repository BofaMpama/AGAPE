about us

Agape is a christian charity organisation that aims to promote the love of God by sharing and giving to those in need. Our focus is especially on those who although are less priveledged(i.e street children) and have no sufficient means of sustenance are often overlooked by government institutions and private groups.

our mission

To bridge the gap in the standard of living between the more and less priveledged in the society.

our vision

To unite the society as one large community where everyone is looked after and no one is left deserted.

what we do


Food distribution

we're on a mission to see as many mouths fed as we can get. our focus is on the less priviledged and street kids.


Welfare packages

we're working on distributing as many welfare packages to as manhy as would need such.


Free educational programmes

we're creating free educational programmes for the street kids who have little to no form of education.
